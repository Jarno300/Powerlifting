BASICS

What is powerlifting?

Powerlifting is the competition of strength training. In general athletes compete to get the highest total weight in a single repetition in the 4 main lifts: bench press, squad, deadlift and overhead press.

Weight & age classes

Athletes compete within a certain class, based on their age and bodyweight.

Weight

Men: 53 - 59 - 66 - 74 - 83 - 93 - 105 - 120 - 120 kg+

Women: 43 - 47 - 52 - 57 - 63 - 72 - 84 - 84 kg+

Age

Sub-junior: 14–18 | Junior: 19–23 | Open: any age | Masters 1: 40–49 | Masters 2 50–59 | Masters 3: 60–69 | Masters 4: 70+

Main events

Competitions are often hosted on a local level, wherever you are in the world. However these are the biggest events, recognized by the Internation Powerlifting Federation:

  • Open Equipped Powerlifting Championships
  • World Masters Powerlifting Championships
  • World Sub-Junior and Junior Powerlifting Championships
  • FISU World University Championship Powerlifting
  • Euro Muscle Show Amsterdam
  • World Open Classic Powerlifting Championships
  • World Open, Sub-Juniors, Juniors, Masters Bench Press Championships
